================================================================================
                            SKINCARE TIPS
================================================================================

1. Always apply skincare products from thinnest to thickest consistency.
2. Don't forget sunscreen - it's the best anti-aging product!
3. Hydration is key - drink plenty of water for glowing skin.
4. Remove makeup before bed to let your skin breathe.
5. Exfoliate 2-3 times a week for smoother skin.
6. Apply eye cream with your ring finger - it's the gentlest!
7. Sheet masks are perfect for instant hydration boosts.
8. Layer your serums for maximum benefits.
9. Night creams work best when applied to damp skin.
10. Don't forget your neck and décolletage in your skincare routine!
11. Wait 1-2 minutes between skincare steps for better absorption.
12. Double cleanse at night to remove all traces of makeup and sunscreen.
13. Use products with Vitamin C in the morning and Retinol at night.
14. Store Vitamin C serums in a cool, dark place to maintain potency.
15. Always patch test new products before full application.
